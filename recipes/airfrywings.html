Air Fried Chicken Wings


Description

Did you forget to thaw the chicken wings and your mom is on her way home? Do you want to save your booty from getting beat? Well you're in luck! You can make chicken wings from frozen in just 40 minutes with only two ingrediants!

Ingrediets

  • Chicken Wings (Frozen)
  • Buffalo Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. Place wings in the air fryer basket
  3. Cook wings for 15 minutes, turn, and cook for 15 more minutes. While the wings are frying, place wing sauce in a small saucepan and heat on low.
  4. Flip wings one more time and cook until cripsy, about 6 more minutes
  5. Transfer wings toa large bowl and pour warmed Buffalo wing sauce over the top. Toss and serve immediately.
